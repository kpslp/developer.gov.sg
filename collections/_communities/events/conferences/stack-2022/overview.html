About STACK Developer Conference

STACK Developer Conference is GovTech's flagship conference for developers, designers, architects and technologists. It gathers the tech community to discuss the latest digital technologies and use cases, and explore technical collaboration opportunities with the public sector.

Call For Proposal

Please submit your content proposals for any of the following themes.

  1. Beyond Cloud: Many organisations including governments are adopting the cloud. Is the cloud journey just about moving workloads to the cloud? What's next? This track explores how cloud-managed services, SaaS and PaaS can help accelerate and improve digital products and services.
  2. Building Digital Trust: Cybersecurity is always in season. Based on a study 69% of organisations are increasing their investments in cybersecurity. Learn about trends in cybersecurity and how the industry and public sector is defending itself through zero trust, confidential computing and advances in tooling.
  3. Fail-Safe App Development: DevSecOps, Observability, Serverless, Microservices? - find out about the latest tools of the trade and best practices to build high-quality and secure products that scale.
  4. Smart City: Vision to Reality: This track showcases how sensors, data and technology can combine to build better living spaces, improve quality of life, public safety and sustainability for citizens and businesses.
  5. Data-fication: Data transformation is at the heart of digitalisation. Every organisation reinventing itself to make better use of tech has to raise its data maturity for the entire organisation. Regardless of where you are in your data journey, this track will help you accelerate to tap the power of data.
  6. Nurturing the Tech Culture & Careers: A critical ingredient to ship a successful product is seldom the technology but great people. This is a track on the culture and practices of tech organisations and how they attract, nurture and retain high-performing technical teams.
  7. Government Track: Closed track for Public Officers to help share best practices and support for internal processes
  8. Tech for Tomorrow: Showcasing emerging tech trends and how that can be tapped for both the public and private sectors.
