Overview

In developing products, growing and enabling great teams is essential. Team coaching enables high value-creation teams to deliver effective solutions. This STACK-X Meetup will equip you with the essential key solution conceptualisation mindset and development skillsets to succeed at creating and supporting impactful products and services. Hear about the GovTech Services Agile Coach Team’s signature Agile Coaching approach, that combines the agile way of working and team coaching to help teams creatively solve complex challenges effectively. The team will also share valuable tips that help to jumpstart the building of collaborative team relationships and to aid in managing of meaningful products and services as well as day-to-day operations. This Meetup includes an activity segment facilitated by experienced trainers, where you can practise applying the coaching approach and use the four-step Enabling Loop guide on common use cases.
