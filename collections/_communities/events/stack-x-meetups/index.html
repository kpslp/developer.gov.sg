Overview for STACK-X Meetups

The STACK-X Meetup community is a platform for everyone who is passionate about using technology for public good. It is hosted on Meetup and provides an avenue for everyone to share their views and feedback, learn from experts and fellow techies and join fruitful discussions on the use of technology for public good. You can also join our STACK-X Meetup community through this link to receive notifications about our upcoming Meetups. Email tmo@tech.gov.sg if you have any enquiries or have something to share with the STACK-X community!
